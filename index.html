About SSKC

The Singapore Shotokan Karate Club is the only affiliation in Singapore of SKIF Japan, founded by the Legendary Soke Hirokazu Kanazawa in 1978. Our Kaicho Michael Wong , 8th Dan SKIF is the affiliation holder for Singapore.
